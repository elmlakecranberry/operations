## Purpose

Ensure personnel entering the chemical storage area do so safely and in compliance with applicable regulations.

## Required PPE

Before entering, gather:

- Chemical-resistant gloves
- Safety glasses or face shield
- Long sleeves and pants (no exposed skin)
- Closed-toe boots
- Respirator (if handling volatile materials)

## Procedure

1. Check the posted chemical inventory sheet on the storage building door
2. Verify you have the appropriate PPE for chemicals present
3. Sign in on the entry log with date, time, and purpose
4. Unlock the storage area using the issued key
5. Prop the door open for ventilation before entering
6. If you detect unusual odors, do not enterâ€”report immediately
7. Retrieve or store materials as needed
8. Close and lock the door upon exit
9. Sign out on the entry log with exit time
10. Return PPE to designated storage area

## If Something Goes Wrong

- **Spill**: Do not attempt cleanup unless trained. Exit and notify the designated safety contact.
- **Exposure**: Move to fresh air. Rinse affected area with water. Seek medical attention if irritation persists.
- **Missing inventory**: Report discrepancy to supervisor immediately.

## Related Documents

- Chemical inventory list (posted on storage building)
- SDS binder (inside storage building)
- Emergency contact list (posted in main shop)
