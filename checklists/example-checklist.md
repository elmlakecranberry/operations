# Daily Pump Station Check

> **This is an example checklist for illustrative purposes. Replace with actual checklist.**

**Location:** Main pump station
**Frequency:** Daily during irrigation season
**Time:** Morning, before irrigation starts

---

## Visual Inspection

- [ ] No visible leaks around pumps or fittings
- [ ] No unusual sounds when pumps are running
- [ ] Pressure gauges reading within normal range
- [ ] No debris in intake screens
- [ ] Building door secure and locked when unattended

## Fluid Levels

- [ ] Fuel tank above 1/4 mark
- [ ] Engine oil at proper level
- [ ] Coolant reservoir at proper level

## Electrical

- [ ] Control panel shows no fault lights
- [ ] Transfer switch in correct position
- [ ] Emergency shutoff accessible and functional

## Log Entry

- [ ] Record pressure readings in logbook
- [ ] Record fuel level in logbook
- [ ] Note any issues or concerns
- [ ] Initial and date the log

---

**If anything is wrong:** Do not start pumps. Contact [designated contact] immediately.

---

*This example demonstrates the expected format for checklists. Actual items will vary based on specific equipment and conditions.*
