# Equipment

Inventory of equipment and implements at Elm Lake Cranberry.

## Guidance

Include any equipment that:

- Is used for farm operations
- Requires maintenance
- Has significant replacement cost

This includes implements, harvesters, sprayers, mowers, and other machinery. Pumps have their own inventory file.

## Column Definitions

- **Name / ID** — How we refer to this equipment
- **Type** — Harvester, sprayer, mower, implement, etc.
- **Make / Model** — Manufacturer and model if known
- **Power Source** — PTO, hydraulic, electric, engine, manual
- **Primary Use** — What this equipment is used for
- **Attached To** — What vehicle/tractor this typically pairs with (if applicable)
- **Status** — Active, needs repair, seasonal only, retired
- **Notes** — Storage location, known issues, special requirements
