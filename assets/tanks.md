# Tanks

Inventory of storage tanks at Elm Lake Cranberry.

## Guidance

Include any tank that:

- Stores fuel, water, or chemicals
- Has significant capacity
- Requires maintenance, inspection, or compliance

## Column Definitions

- **Name / ID** — How we refer to this tank
- **Location** — Physical location on the property
- **Contents** — Propane, diesel, gasoline, water, fertilizer, etc.
- **Capacity** — Total capacity (gallons, pounds, etc.)
- **Owned / Leased** — Who owns the tank
- **Supplier** — Who fills this tank (if applicable)
- **Last Inspection** — Date of last inspection (if required)
- **Status** — Active, empty, decommissioned
- **Notes** — Delivery access issues, fill schedule, compliance requirements

## Relationship to Fuel Coordination

Tanks listed here that contain fuel should also appear in [fuel-and-power-coordination.md](../energy/fuel-and-power-coordination.md) with delivery and runway information.
