# Vehicles

Inventory of vehicles at Elm Lake Cranberry.

## Guidance

Include any vehicle that:

- Is titled or registered
- Requires fuel and maintenance
- Is used for farm operations

For each vehicle, document its primary use, fuel type, and current status.

## Column Definitions

- **Name / ID** — How we refer to this vehicle (e.g., "Red Truck", "Tractor 1", or plate/unit number)
- **Type** — Truck, tractor, ATV, UTV, trailer, etc.
- **Year** — Model year
- **Make / Model** — Manufacturer and model
- **Fuel Type** — Diesel, gasoline, propane, electric
- **Primary Use** — What this vehicle is typically used for
- **Licensed** — Yes/No (for road-legal vehicles)
- **Status** — Active, needs repair, seasonal only, retired
- **Notes** — Anything else relevant (VIN location, known issues, etc.)
