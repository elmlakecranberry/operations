# Land Parcels

Inventory of land parcels owned or leased by Elm Lake Cranberry.

## Guidance

Include any parcel that:

- Is owned or leased by Elm Lake Cranberry
- Has a distinct tax parcel number
- Is used for farm operations, access, or buffer

## Column Definitions

- **Parcel ID** — Internal name or identifier we use
- **Tax Key / PIN** — Official parcel identification number from the county
- **Acres** — Total acreage
- **Municipality** — Town, village, or city where parcel is located
- **Ownership** — Owned, leased, easement, etc.
- **Primary Use** — Cranberry beds, reservoir, upland, woods, residence, etc.
- **Zoning** — Current zoning classification
- **Access** — How the parcel is accessed (road name, easement, landlocked)
- **Notes** — Legal description reference, deed book/page, known encumbrances
