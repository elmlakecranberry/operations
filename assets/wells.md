# Wells

Inventory of water supply wells at Elm Lake Cranberry.

## Guidance

Include any well that:

- Supplies water for irrigation, frost protection, or domestic use
- Is permitted or regulated
- Requires maintenance or monitoring

## Column Definitions

- **Name / ID** — How we refer to this well
- **Location** — Physical location on the property
- **Type** — Drilled, dug, point, etc.
- **Depth** — Total depth in feet
- **Capacity (GPM)** — Tested or estimated yield
- **Primary Use** — Irrigation, domestic, frost protection, etc.
- **Permit #** — DNR or other permit number if applicable
- **Pump Installed** — Yes/No — reference pumps.md for pump details
- **Status** — Active, seasonal, capped, abandoned
- **Notes** — Water quality issues, permit conditions, maintenance history
